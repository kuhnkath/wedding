Getting Here

For our loved ones travelling from abroad — here's everything you need to navigate Stockholm.

✈️ From Arlanda Airport

Stockholm Arlanda Airport (ARN) is the main international airport, approximately 40 minutes from the city centre.

  • Pendeltåg (Commuter train) — ~40 min. Price: ~292 SEK (250 + 42 SEK Arlanda supplement)
  • Flygbussarna — Airport coach with several stops in the city. ~45 min. Book here →
  • Arlanda Express — Fastest option to city centre, 20 min. ~SEK 299
  • Taxi / Uber — Estimate: 400–600 SEK to city centre
